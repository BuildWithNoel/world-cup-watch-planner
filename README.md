# world-cup-watch-planner
An interactive FIFA World Cup 2026 planner that converts match times to any UTC timezone, helps fans determine which matches they can watch, and provides personalized viewing coverage through manual selection, Early Waker, Late Sleeper, and Custom Schedule modes.

World Cup Watch Planner is a fan-built tool designed to help football supporters maximize their FIFA World Cup 2026 experience. Select your timezone, explore the complete tournament schedule, and instantly see which matches fit your lifestyle. Whether you're an early riser, a night owl, or have a custom weekly routine, the planner calculates your tournament coverage and highlights the matches you can realistically watch.
